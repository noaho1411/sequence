The aim of the game is to click the buttons in the same order as they flash.

The more answers you get correct, the longer the amount of buttons per question gets.

If you get one button wrong you lose.

The emojis at the top represent your current answer, and by pressing the smiling face emoji you can toggle an overlay of which emoji is which button.

In order to reset your answer if you haven't submitted it yet, you can press the reset button and your answer will be cleared.

Your answer will be submitted on the last button click (you have to remember how many buttons there are) so be careful with your last click!

Note: some of these features are disabled in challenge mode.
